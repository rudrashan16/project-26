INGREDIENTS

for jamun:
¾ cup (100 grams) milk powder, unsweetened
½ cup (60 grams) maida / plain flour
½ tsp baking powder
2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
milk (for kneading)
ghee or oil (for frying)

for sugar syrup:
2 cup sugar
2 cup water
2 cardamom
¼ tsp saffron / kesar
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp rose water
